Site Philosophy

Â  This site exists to scratch an itch I’ve long had. Sharing stuff on social media has never been my mojo, I’ll often post my photography, however I yearned for something more expansive and interesting, so I made a website.

Â  The design of this site was made with one goal in mind: create vibrant a site that looks good and is fun to use, with the smallest file size possible. This meant several things, for one, no javascript but also no media queries to change the layout. Fonts are used, but are extremely slimmed down to reduce filesize. Combined with optimising images, it means even the largest page on the site is under 800kb and the smallest is just 45kb.
