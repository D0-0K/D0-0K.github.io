Origami Modular Festive Tree

The Origami festive tree is a modular origami tree with little slots in the leaves to put whatever you want! It was designed as a modular, flat-packable festive tree that could be practical, allowing you to put different decorations or items in it. The design still needs some tweaking before I’m happy with posting instructions, I’ll probably get around to it soon.
