Rockbox Theming

  • ipod
  • rockbox

In recent years, there have been many developments in the rockbox theming space. New ways of abstracting the theme tags rockbox uses to customise it’s UI have been created to give users even more functionality and better themes than ever before. However, a lot of these tricks are not well documented and involve being quite familiar with how rockbox theming works in order to extrapolate their construction. This guide intends to be a collection of these tricks, how to make them, as well as discussing some newer or rarely-used tags in rockbox.
