Image Compression Tests

Requirements

- Images must be smaller than the current ImageMagick script. - The maximum resolution must be 720x480. - Images must have colour to maintain readability.

Hypothesis

Two possible routes come to mind immediately. The first would be using a modern image format, likely webp for now, however this method would leave users on older browsers stuck with whatever alternative. The other route might actually provide a nice alternative, or be just as good on it’s own. Reducing the colours of an image, and using the gif image format. Since gifs are indexed, having less colours would mean smaller filesizes. However my previous tests with gifs didn’t give me too much hope in it’s ability to be small. The ultimate goal with these tests will be to come to some clear conclusions on various methods of making small images.
