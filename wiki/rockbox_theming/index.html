Rockbox Theming for Beginners

Learn how to make your own iPod themes from scratch, using just a text editor. No experience of any sort required, all you’ll need is an iPod or similar device running Rockbox and a computer to create the theme on. By the end you’ll not only have a working theme, but also the skills to go and design your own themes.
