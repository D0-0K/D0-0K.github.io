Digicam Photography

I recently picked up an old Sony DSC-S85 digicam, a camera roughly 3 months younger than me, to see if these long-forgotten pioneers of the digital camera age were still relevant. Surprisingly, the photos they produce are crisp and well defined. The camera can also take very low resolution videos and gifs, which have an artistic flair to them. Sadly the battery is the most disappointing part, but I think it could be worth attempting to replace or upgrade in time.
