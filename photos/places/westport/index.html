Westport

In the summer of 2018, I spent some time in the town of Westport in the west of Ireland. Staying near the bay, the docks as well Westport House were a short walk from where we stayed. The views never failed to disappoint, being at the foot of Croagh Patrick. My favourite memory from here is going off the main path in Westport House, following a dirt trail and coming across a gorgeous red bridge before a short waterfall.
